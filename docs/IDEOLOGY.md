## **CULTURAL CONSIDERATIONS**

### **South African Taxi Industry Dynamics**
- **Community-based transport**: Taxis are lifelines for communities, not just transport
- **Economic empowerment**: Many drivers are small business owners supporting families
- **Route territories**: Established routes and territories with complex social dynamics
- **Cash economy**: Predominantly cash-based transactions with informal negotiations
- **Safety concerns**: Passengers often wait in unsafe areas, drivers face route uncertainties

### **App Impact on Traditional System**
- **Transparency without disruption**: Enhances existing system rather than replacing it
- **Empowers existing players**: Helps current drivers and passengers make better decisions
- **Reduces uncertainty**: Less waiting time for passengers, better route planning for drivers
- **Maintains human interaction**: Technology facilitates but doesn't replace personal connections
- **Respects territorial dynamics**: Shows availability without imposing route changes

### **Strategic Intelligence Benefits**
